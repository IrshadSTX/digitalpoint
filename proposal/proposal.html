Introduction
This proposal outlines the complete scope of work, deliverables, pricing, and timeline for the design, development, and deployment of a professional landing website for Digital Point — Kerala's trusted B.Ed & M.Ed educational consultancy based in Areekode, Malappuram.

The website is designed to generate admissions enquiries, showcase the DiGi Learning App, and establish Digital Point as the most credible B.Ed/M.Ed consultancy in the region.
